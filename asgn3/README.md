# CSE 160 Assignment 1: Painting
## Written by Johnny Fisher
## Animal: Crocodile. 

Notes to Grader:  
- I didn't have graph paper so I drew in the vertical lines on my line paper, which are a bit uneven but get the job done. I changed a bit from the crocodile I drew on the paper when putting it in webgl because I didn't like how some of the boxes looked. The feet are triangles instead of boxes, etc.  
- For the awesomness component I added in 2 sliders: Opacity and Spin.  
- Opacity changes the transparency of any shape you add. Doesn't work for the crocodile.
- Spin changes the rotation that points and triangles have when placed. I had to turn points into a shape made of two triangles for this to work.  
- The crocodile is placed wherever the previous shape was placed and disappears if you place a new shape. It also copies the spin of the previously placed shape.
